Other Hardwares



Intel Core i7-6700K

The i7-6700K is a beast when it comes to performance. Running at a base of 4.00GHz and has overclocking capabilities, this CPU is both for gamers and workspace enthusiasts. I still haven't overclocked mine because I don't have a reason to and I mainly use mine for gaming. My maximum temperature reading when gaming is around 65-68 degree Celsius. Those are good numbers especially when factoring in ambient room temperatures in the country and my MasterLiquid 120 liquid cooler from Cooler Master.

NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970

Being the minimum requirement to have VR-ready gear, the GTX 970 is one of the mid-range budget performers that one can buy on the market. I don't use it for VR because I have no money to purchase a VR machine, but for even modern game titles this GPU performs well. I often get around 77-81 degrees Celsius when gaming. My huge concern is the bottleneck between the i7-6700K and the GTX 970. My CPU is only running at around 40% performance when my GPU is running at max capacity, wasting the untapped 60% CPU performance.

Corsair Vengeance 16GB (2x8GB) DDR4 DRAM

16GB of RAM is overkill for most users, but I personally like running multiple applications all at the same time, thus making 16GB a sweet spot for my setup.

250GB Samsung 850 EVO SSD

I use my SSD as a boot up drive for faster boot times. I also put my most played games here in order to have faster load times and ultimately a smoother gaming experience. All hail SSDs.
